Our Story

We have a very long history of helping people get where they need to go. We began as a small airline, starting in the 1920's with some of the earliest travel planes created. Our modest company quickly began to grow bigger, earning more customers as airplanes and technology rapidly improved.

With more competition in the industry but still a desire to help people, the owner decided to turn our airline into one of the biggest travel agencies. Even though we no longer run the airports themself, we still love to make sure people reach their destinations and have the greatest time possible!
